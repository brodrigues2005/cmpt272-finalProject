About the Metro Vancouver Emergency Reporting System

This system allows users to report emergencies in Metro Vancouver and view emergency locations on a map. Developed for enhanced accessibility to emergency services.

Our Mission

At Metro Vancouver Emergency, we aim to create a safer environment by empowering the community with real-time access to emergency information and resources. We believe that awareness and quick reporting can significantly enhance safety and response times.

Features of the System

  • Real-time emergency reporting for the public
  • Interactive map with live incident tracking
  • Easy access for emergency responders and citizens alike
  • Comprehensive data and insights on current emergencies

Emergency Tips

  • Fire: Move to safety, avoid inhaling smoke, and call for help immediately.
  • Medical Emergency: Stay calm, apply basic first aid if possible, and report the incident.
  • Vehicle Accident: Move to a safe location, avoid traffic, and notify authorities.
